Piracy, especially online, can expose your private information. Illegally downloading anything, especially through peer-to-peer networks like torrents, can lead to malware infections, data breaches, and even identity theft. A torrent site, also known as a torrent tracker or BitTorrent tracker, is a website that hosts and indexes torrent files. These sites allow users to find and download files, including movies, TV shows, software, and more, using the BitTorrent protocol. The BitTorrent protocol is a protocol that allows users to download and stream content from multiple sources simultaneously rather than a single source. Websites like torrents are known to often be infected with malware that can extract a user’s personal data.
