Legal issues

Piracy involves the unauthorized copying, distribution, and use of copyrighted material, which is illegal. Copyright holders have the right to control how their work is used and distributed. Piracy can lead to financial losses for these stakeholders, impacting their ability to create and distribute content in the future. Piracy can also lead to fines and imprisonment for those who attempt it.
