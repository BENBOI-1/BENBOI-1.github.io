Social issues

Some people pirate content because they are unable to afford the legal option or because the content is not available in their country. Some also argue that piracy allows them to try content before actually buying it, saving money and in some cases generating more money for creators from people who want the features the game can only offer from a legal purchase. A lot of people view piracy as some type of rebellion against large corporations. They believe that information should be more freely available and not paywalled by corperations who do not need the money.
