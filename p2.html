There are many digital systems that can enable piracy/be used to pirate content. The most common are electronics like computers, though piracy originated through videocassettes. Nowadays, digital piracy is more common and accessible. - The key stakeholders 1. The major stakeholders in terms of piracy include: 2. Content creators (Authors, Musicians, Artists, Filmmakers.) 3. Consumers (individuals who consume pirated media.) 4. Rightsholders (publishers and distributors) 5. The public. A stake holder is an individual, group, organization, community or other interested party that is affected by a decision made in relation to that business, group, organization, community etc.
